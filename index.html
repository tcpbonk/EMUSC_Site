Ways to Support EMUSC

EMUSC is a student organization at Eastern Michigan University that operates for zero profit. Our goal is to provide a wecoming, supportive, fun, and exciting environment to all smashers near Ypsilanti. We work tirelessly to host events, run streams, create content, secure prizing, and provide a learning environment for our players purely for the love of our local scene and Super Smash Bros. Any support is greatly appreciated and will go towards more growth for the EMUSC community.

  1. Come to our events - If you are able to physically come to our events, then you directly help to contribute to our clubs growth and success!
  2. Be a good sport - Toxicity, both online and in person only drives players away from our community. 
  3. Use your Twitch Prime sub on our channel - We are Twitch affiliates, and your Twitch Prime subscription can help us get a couple extra dollars per month for club equipment. Our Twitch is https://www.twitch.tv/emusc
  4. Buy that merch. - We have shirts, hoodies, and other apparel available here. We make it as cheap as we can, but if there are any proceeds, they will go towards our club. Be sure to join our Discord to stay up to date on our merch!
  5. Donations are always appreciated - We don't charge any fees for any of our club events and we never will, but donations are still much appreciated! It all goes into a club PayPal and we are transparent with our spending. It helps us to get equipment necessary to run our club! Feel free to donate at this link.
