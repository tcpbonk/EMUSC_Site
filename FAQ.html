EMUSC FAQ's

So a lot of people have been wandering...

  • Can non-students participate in EMUSC tournaments? - Yes! All are welcome to attend EMUSC events. The only exception for this rule is if EMU provides prizing and specifies that they only want students to enter. All free events organized by EMUSC will be open to all.
  • Do your tournaments cost money? Due to school regulations, our events are all free and do not have a prize pool, unless it is provided by the school or a partner. 
  • Do I have to enter the tournament to come? No! We have many players who come to our meetings just to hang out and play friendly, casual matches.
  • Where/when is are your events? - We will keep our EagleSync page up to date with all of our tournament times, for additional meetings and events join our Discord server to stay connected. Check here!
  • Do I have to have a Switch/GameCube/Controller to play? - No you do not have to bring a console, controller, or setup. There will almost always be someone willing to lend you a controller and there will be setups that you can play at. It is HIGHLY appreciated if you have access to any of this equipment to bring it, though.  Be sure to check out Facebook marketplace, craigslist, or eBay for some deals on equipment. 
  • What Smash game do you guys play? Mainly, we play Smash Ultimate, but we also play Melee, and P+. Events are organized for Ultimate and Melee. However, if you take initiative and bring any other Smash game, I'm sure you will find others to play it with you.
  • How do I start playing smash? Literally just pick up a controller and have fun! Many of our members are willing to help new players learn! If you are interested in learning the competitive basics, try to do some research on YouTube. Here is a good starting place for Smash Ultimate and here is one for Melee.
