About EMUSC

Our Club's Description:

EMUâ€™s Super Smash Bros Club provides players of all skill level, both casual and competitive, with a fun, supportive, and learning environment to play and learn more about Super Smash Brothers and to build lasting friendships.
We hope to give people the opportunity to play all the games that are in the Super Smash Bros. franchise from Smash 64 to Smash Ultimate. The availability of these games will vary based on how many setups are brought but feel free to bring a setup of any game!
Because of the number of people that come to meetings, we encourage players to bring their own controllers and setups (TV, console, and accompanying Super Smash Brothers game) if they are able to and extra if applicable. If a player does not have a controller to a game that they would like to play, don't worry! Members are very open to letting others use their controllers. Just be mindful and respectful of other people's property!
Every week we will have a casual get together with friendly matches and also run tournaments for those who wish to enter! 

Our Club's Leadership

Our club is lead by an executive board of students, overseen by a faculty advisor. Our executive board is elected by members of the club. For an up to date version of our executive board please visit our page on EagleSync, where EMU officially tracks student organization leadership.
